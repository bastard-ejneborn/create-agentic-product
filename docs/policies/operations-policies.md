## Secondary Shelf Life (Sekundära hållbarhetstider)

### Purpose
Define acceptable storage times and conditions for opened products to ensure food safety, quality, and guest experience across all Bastard Burgers restaurants.

### Scope
All 74 Bastard Burgers restaurants. Applies to all staff handling food products during preparation, storage, and service.

### Policy — Basic Rules

- **Primary shelf life**: The period from production and packaging of a product until it first becomes unacceptable under defined environmental conditions.
- **Secondary shelf life**: The time after opening a package during which a food product maintains an acceptable quality level.
- **Planning requirement**: Carefully plan purchases and production against sales. Failure to follow this leads to food waste, degraded end product, and poor guest experience.

### Key Temperature Reference

| Temperature | Use For |
|-------------|---------|
| **Min -18°C** | Frozen storage |
| **Max +4°C** | Raw meat (rolled meat, thawed meat) |
| **Max +6°C** | Thawed protein products (Beyond meat, chicken, nuggets, sticks, etc.) |
| **Max +8°C** | Dressings, sauces, vegetables, bacon, cheese |
| **Min +65°C** | Hot-hold (fried bacon, hot melted cheese — fried/heated to 72°C first) |
| **Room temp** | Bread, room-temp sauces (ketchup, mustard, BBQ in bottles) |

### Key Terminology

- **Marking (Märkning)**: Total shelf life after opening — marked on container/package with date. Product must be discarded when marking expires.
- **Timer**: Active service time — how long the product can stay in service position (on the line, in the hot-hold) before being replaced.
- **Upptining (Thawing)**: Time required to safely thaw a frozen product (typically 24 hours under refrigeration).

### Procedures

1. **On receipt**: Check temperature of delivery. Reject products outside acceptable temperature ranges.
2. **On opening**: Mark the product with date/time of opening. Set both marking (shelf life) and timer (service time) as applicable.
3. **During service**: Monitor timer — replace products when service timer expires even if marking is still valid.
4. **Cooling hot products**: Stekt lök (fried onion) must be cooled to minimum +8°C within 2 hours.
5. **Heating**: Hot products (bacon, melted cheese) must reach 72°C before being stored at hot-hold temperature (min +65°C).
6. **Thawing**: Frozen products must be thawed under refrigeration for 24 hours — never at room temperature.
7. **Discard**: Any product exceeding marking date OR service timer must be discarded immediately. No exceptions.
